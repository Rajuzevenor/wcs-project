About World Cyber Shield

World Cyber Shield (WCS) is a cybersecurity awareness platform created to educate users about digital safety, fraud prevention, and cyber threats. Cybercrime is rising rapidly in today’s digital era.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

  • Increase in phishing scams
  • Online banking fraud
  • Identity theft risks
  • Social engineering attacks

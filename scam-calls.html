How to Identify Scam Calls in India

Published: March 2026 | By WCS Team

Cybercrime in India is increasing rapidly. Fraudsters use psychological tricks, fear tactics, and fake identities to steal money and personal information. Understanding how scam calls work is the first step in protecting yourself.

Common Types of Scam Calls

  • Fake bank verification calls asking for OTP
  • KYC update fraud calls
  • Lottery or prize-winning scams
  • Police or government impersonation scams
  • Loan approval or job offer scams

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Caller creates urgency or panic
  • Asks for OTP, ATM PIN, or UPI details
  • Requests remote access to your phone
  • Threatens account suspension
  • Offers unrealistic rewards
Important: Banks and government agencies never ask for OTP, password, or PIN over phone calls.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Do not share sensitive information on calls.
  • Verify official numbers from bank websites.
  • Use call-blocking and fraud detection apps.
  • Report fraud to Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).

Conclusion

Staying calm and aware can prevent financial loss. Cybersecurity awareness is the strongest defense against fraud.

Top 5 Mobile Security Tips for 2026

Follow these important steps to protect your smartphone:

  • Install apps only from official Play Store or trusted sources.
  • Keep your phone updated with latest security patches.
  • Avoid clicking unknown links in SMS or WhatsApp.
  • Use strong passwords and enable fingerprint or face lock.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
